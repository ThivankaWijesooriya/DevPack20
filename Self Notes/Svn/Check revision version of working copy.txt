
****Method 1

Right-click on the working directory in windows explorer, 
and select "Properties" (Not TortoiseSVN->Properties). 
You will see the Properties dialog, which will have a tab called "Subversion". 
Click on it, and you will see the version number, 
and other info.


****Method 2

TortoiseSVN -> Show log: The line in bold marks the current revision in your working copy.
